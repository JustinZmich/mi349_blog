Hi all! My name is Justin, and I'm a senior at Michigan State University and I'm studying Information Science. One day, I hope to have my own business that designs custom software and technology for emerging industries.

Right now, I'm working on a project to develop an online community for senior citizens. This community will provide access to recources for seniors, and will cater specifically to the needs of the elderly. The project is called Senior Speak Cafe and I'm excited to work on this project with the help of some others. This site is nowhere near a representation of what the final project will look like, but I wanted to use this as an exercise in setting up a basic, static HTML blog.
